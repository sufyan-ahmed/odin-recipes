Fried Rice

Simple, tasty fried rice

Quick and easy fried rice made with leftover rice, eggs, and veggies.

Customize with protein or extra vegetables as desired.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Use day-old cooked rice (cold helps prevent clumping).
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add beaten eggs and scramble; set aside.
  4. Sauté chopped onion, garlic, and mixed vegetables until tender.
  5. Add the rice and stir-fry, breaking up any clumps.
  6. Return the eggs to the pan and mix with the rice and veggies.
  7. Season with soy sauce, a splash of sesame oil, and salt/pepper to taste.
  8. Stir-fry until everything is well combined and heated through; garnish and serve.
