Delicious Homemade Pizza

Enjoy this classic pizza recipe with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and melted cheese.

Perfect for sharing with friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Shred the cheese and sprinkle it over the sauce.
  6. Add the pepperoni slices on top.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
